About Me

I am a Fashion Design and Merchandising student based in Manila, with a strong foundation in creative direction, branding, and editorial production. My experience includes internships at Vogue Philippines and Mich Dulce Studio, where I gained hands-on exposure to fashion media, styling, garment development, and couture-level craftsmanship.


My academic and project-based work spans across design, marketing, and video direction, reflecting a multidisciplinary approach to fashion. With a focus on storytelling, identity, and execution, I aim to create thoughtful, visually cohesive work that bridges creativity and strategy.
